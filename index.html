OUR CHANNEL

Science for kids by Kids. A fun, weekly DIY science experiment show hosted by best friends Riley and Toby, along with their Science Kitchen Crew. The boys and their channels content creatively explores the facts why's and the how's of DIY science at home. With various quirky segments from in and around the kitchen, the show aims to present science in a fun to watch unique way!

With the channels unofficial motto of "Science is Everywhere", the content is a not too serious way of inspiring the real life long learning of science!
